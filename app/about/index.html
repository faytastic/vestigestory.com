VESTIGE

THE MESSAGE WITHIN

Each season, we tell a tale of two individuals, brought together by a message in a bottle. In a time and age where communication is sparse and undervalued, we showcase how inspiring and precious connecting with others can be. Every story works to bring two seemingly different souls together, forming a harmonious collection.

Finding meaning goes beyond looking pretty. We strive for lasting impact. Our designs only use premium, natural fabrics that can stand the test of time. Our story continues from season to season, for we love continuity. We also support our local manufacturers. Some of our pieces are made in our hometown, Vancouver B.C.
